Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tracker really free?

Yes! Our core features are predominantly free for individual users. We believe financial literacy should be accessible to everyone, so you can track expenses, debts, and savings without paying a dime.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can export all your transaction history to CSV at any time from your settings panel. Your data belongs to you, always.

Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption for data storage and never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers. Your financial privacy is our top priority.
